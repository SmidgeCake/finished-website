What is it about?

IT and Digital Media Level 1 is a great starting point for people looking to progress onto a Level 2 course or apprenticeship. It includes several practical learning opportunities. For example, as part of the course, I had the opportunity to go on a trip to Yamazaki Mazak, where we toured and learned about their technology. During the course, there are many fun projects to do as well as the normal units, such as Introduction to Coding, Computing Hardware, and more.

This would be the perfect course for anybody with an interest in starting to learn about IT or Digital Media. The course is assessed with a number of assignments, and you will receive support from college facilities with your studies, emotional well-being, and advice on how to get where you want to in the future.

What do you need to apply?

To apply to this course, you will need to be 16-18 years old without having achieved 4 GCSEs at grade 4 and above.
The course contains 20 hours of work experience, Thrive and PSD sessions to help with personal development, and Maths and English Functional Skills/GCSE. After completing this course, you can either progress onto a Level 2 course or an apprenticeship.
